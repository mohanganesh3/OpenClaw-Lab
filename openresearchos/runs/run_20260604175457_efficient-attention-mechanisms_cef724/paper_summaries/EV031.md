## Core Claim

This paper presents a novel unsupervised multiview sparse filtering method to effectively extract fault-related features from raw current signals for wind turbine gearbox diagnosis.

## Limitations

- Difficulty in extracting informative features from raw current signals due to dominant fundamental components, harmonics, and electrical noise.
