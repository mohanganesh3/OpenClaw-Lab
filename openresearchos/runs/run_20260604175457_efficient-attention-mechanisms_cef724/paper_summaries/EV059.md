## Core Claim

We propose SIMPLE, a new structured pruning framework for generative PLMs that targets persistent outliers in the shared hidden dimension to improve efficiency.

## Limitations

- The method is specifically designed for generative PLMs, and its effectiveness on other types of PLMs is not investigated.
