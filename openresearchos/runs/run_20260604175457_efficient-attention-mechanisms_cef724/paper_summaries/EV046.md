## Core Claim

We propose MoCoBE-SD, a new network to alleviate data sparsity and noise in sequential recommendation by providing rich informative supervisions from both sequence-level and item-level perspectives.

## Limitations

- The method is specifically designed for sparse and noisy data, and its effectiveness on dense, clean datasets is not demonstrated.
