## Core Claim

We propose a meta-learning framework that can rapidly generate high-performing neural network architectures for new tasks with minimal data and computation.

## Limitations

- The meta-training phase is computationally expensive and requires a large number of tasks.
- The method's performance may degrade when adapting to tasks that are significantly different from the meta-training distribution.
