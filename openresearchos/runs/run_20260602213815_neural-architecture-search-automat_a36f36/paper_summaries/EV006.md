## Core Claim

This paper proposes a Neural Architecture Search (NAS) method to create a hybrid model that combines the global feature understanding of Vision Transformers with the local detail accuracy of Convolutional Neural Networks for improved pedestrian detection in crowded scenes.

## Limitations

- CNNs have a narrow receptive field and cannot infer occlusion.
- Vision Transformers tend to ignore local features.
