## Core Claim

This paper proposes a specialized pre-training recipe, CXRMIM, to adapt Masked Image Modeling for the challenging domain of chest X-ray images.

## Limitations

- Standard MIM is challenging to apply to chest X-rays.
- Chest radiographs are fundamentally different from natural photographic images.
