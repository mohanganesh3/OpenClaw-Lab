## Core Claim

This paper proposes a Supervised Detail-Guided Multiscale State-Space Model to overcome the limitations of existing deep learning pan-sharpening methods, such as poor upsampling and insufficient multiscale feature exploitation.

## Limitations

- Vanilla LR-MS upsampling ignores domain knowledge and discards crucial information.
- Failure to fully exploit multiscale spatial information in remote sensing images.
