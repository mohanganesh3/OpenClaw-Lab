## Core Claim

This paper proposes a Dual Routing Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) model that dynamically selects between local (fine-grained) and global (contextual) representations extracted from speech and text encoders.

## Limitations

- Existing methods rely on single-scale representations that fail to capture the hierarchical structure of emotional signals.
