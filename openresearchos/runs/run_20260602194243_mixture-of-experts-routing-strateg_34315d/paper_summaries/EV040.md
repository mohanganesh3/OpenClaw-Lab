## Core Claim

MedVLMoE is a sparse Mixture of Experts (MoE) Vision Language Model designed to better capture visually complex disease signatures by routing representations to specialized disease expert networks.

## Limitations

- Existing VLMs use a single shared representation space, which is insufficient for capturing the visually complex signatures of different disease classes.
