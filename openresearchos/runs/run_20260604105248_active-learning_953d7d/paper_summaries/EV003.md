## Core Claim

This paper presents a spatio-temporal Cokriging (ST-Cokriging) method for assimilating remote sensing data sets acquired by multiple remote sensing systems with different temporal sampling frequencies and different spatial resolutions.

## Limitations

- No single satellite remote sensing system can provide both high spatial and high temporal resolution data due to the trade-off between revisit frequency and sensor resolution.
