## Abstract

This paper designs a method of learning-based model predictive control for quadrotor UAVs. We use Gaussian process(GP) to build a non-parametric perturbation or unmodelled UAV dynamics online, and model predictive control (MPC) provides a fixed window length of predicted states. In this limited prediction sequence, the best control data points are selected using Entropy Power as a criterion to drive the system in a direction that reduces the uncertainty of the system modeling. Evaluating the relationship between sampled data and closed-loop system performance, improving the effectiveness of data exploration, and reducing computational burden are all crucial for data-driven controller design. The proposed method is validated by simulation. The effectiveness and feasibility of the proposed method are verified through the comparison of various methods.
