An automated pipeline using LLMs to generate research papers across different scientific domains, with automated reviewing and iteration.

## Limitations

- Llama-3.1 405b performs poorly
- Missing sections and results in generated papers
- Small-scale, non-compute-intensive templates
- Low GPU utilization
