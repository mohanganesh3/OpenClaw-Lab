## Core Claim

The paper proposes a novel approach for the enhanced cross-stage feature matching network (ECFNet) to improve weak target detection in infrared images by introducing pixel expansion branching and a feature attention enhancement module (FAEM) to overcome low-resolution limitations.

## Limitations

- Low pixel count and resolution in infrared images
- Significant blurring of boundary information for small targets
