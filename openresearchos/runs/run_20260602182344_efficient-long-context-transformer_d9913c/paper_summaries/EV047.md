## Core Claim

The paper proposes the Dynamic Topology-Aware Linear Attention Network (DTALAN) to efficiently solve the Traveling Salesman Problem by capturing dynamic local geometry and reducing computational complexity.

## Limitations

- Standard linear embedding layers struggle to capture dynamic local geometric relationships between nodes.
- The quadratic complexity of self-attention in the decoder hinders efficiency in large-scale TSP instances.
