## Core Claim

Multimodal emotion recognition systems with explainable AI are necessary to create more robust and effective human-computer interaction in smart environments.

## Limitations

- Limited robustness of single-modality models
- Sensitivity to noise in single-modality models
- Inability to generalize across diverse real-world conditions
- Inefficient feature fusion strategies in existing multimodal approaches
- High computational overhead of existing multimodal approaches
