## Abstract

The increasing risks of fire hazards, toxic gas leaks, and environmental obstacles necessitate intelligent autonomous safety systems. This study presents the Intelligent Rover, an AI-powered robotic platform with obstacle avoidance and gas and fire detection capabilities. For real-time autonomous danger mitigation, it incorporates multi- sensor fusion employing flame sensors, MQ gas sensors, and ultrasonic sensors. The rover carries out adaptive course planning and risk assessment with the help of an integrated microcontroller and AI-based decision-making. Instant notifications and remote monitoring are made possible via a wireless module. The rover performs well in hazardous and industrial settings thanks to deep learning and sensor navigation. Comparing testing to current safety robots reveal superior risk management, faster reaction times, and enhanced danger recognition, by providing real-time hazard warnings, adaptive navigation, and scalable deployment, the Intelligent Rover raises the bar for security and safety.
