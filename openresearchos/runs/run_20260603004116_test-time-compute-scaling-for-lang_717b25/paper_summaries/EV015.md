## Core Claim

We present MCSim, a discrete-event simulator designed specifically for empirical trace-driven multi-cloud network analysis.

## Limitations

- Lack of support for cloud-native hierarchies (providers, regions, zones)
- Inability to incorporate empirical inter-cloud measurements
- No support for egress cost modeling
