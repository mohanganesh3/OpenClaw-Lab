## Problem
Developing AI models remains a heavily manual process that is challenging for natural scientists without specialized AI engineering expertise.

## Core Claim
AIBuildAI-2, a knowledge-enhanced agent with an external, evolving knowledge system, can automatically build AI models at a level competitive with expert human practitioners.

## Key Contributions
- AIBuildAI-2 introduces a hierarchical knowledge system that organizes curated AI development knowledge into high-level instructions and low-level documents.
- The system evolves from the agent's own experience by distilling completed runs into structured takeaways.
- AIBuildAI-2 achieves top performance on benchmarks, outperforming existing baseline methods and competing with expert human practitioners.

## Method
AIBuildAI-2 uses a hierarchical sub-agent design with a dynamically loaded knowledge system that is initialized from web-scraped AI development documents and continually evolves from the agent's own experience.
- Components: Hierarchical knowledge system with high-level instructions and low-level documents, Dynamic context loading based on current state and AI task, Experience distillation mechanism that writes structured takeaways back into the knowledge system
- Key innovation: The key innovation is the evolving hierarchical knowledge system that allows the agent to ground its decisions in concrete, externally verifiable expertise and continuously improve from its own experience.
- Assumptions: The web-scraped documents provide sufficient coverage of AI development knowledge; The agent can effectively distill its experience into structured takeaways; The hierarchical organization of knowledge enables effective context loading
