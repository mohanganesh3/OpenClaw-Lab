## Core Claim

We developed a semi-automated framework for multi-label classification of IHC images to provide a reliable model for annotating cell type-specific protein expression patterns in human tissues.

## Limitations

- Manual annotation is expensive and prone to errors/inter-observer variability.
- Confidence in AI predictions needs to be addressed.
