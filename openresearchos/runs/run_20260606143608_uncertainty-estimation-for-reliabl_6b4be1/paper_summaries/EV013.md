## Core Claim

The paper proposes an active interaction approach for autonomous driving where the ego vehicle's behavior is designed to improve confidence in other vehicles' intention estimations.

## Limitations

- The approach is challenged when predictions of other vehicles' intentions are highly uncertain (approximate equiprobability), potentially leading to conservative or unsafe decisions.
