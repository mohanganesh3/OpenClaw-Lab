## Core Claim

AIBuildAI-2 is a knowledge-enhanced agent with an external, hierarchical knowledge system that dynamically loads relevant context for AI model building, initialized with web-collected AI development documents and continually evolving through distilled experience from completed tasks.

## Limitations

- The performance of AI agents is limited by the static, often outdated, and sparse parametric knowledge of their underlying large language models, particularly on practical AI model engineering know-how
