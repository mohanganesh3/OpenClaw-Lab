## Core Claim

The `skewlmm` R package provides a method for fitting linear mixed models that accommodate skewness and heavy tails, addressing the robustness issues of standard normal-based models.

## Limitations

- Lack of robustness and invalid statistical inferences due to restrictive normality assumptions in standard linear mixed models.
