## Core Claim

The JJ-VMed framework provides a unified system for automatically extracting key medical concepts, generating descriptive image captions, and offering explainability for medical images.

## Limitations

- The framework's performance may be limited by the quality and diversity of the training dataset, and the explainability module's outputs might not always be fully faithful to the underlying model's reasoning.
