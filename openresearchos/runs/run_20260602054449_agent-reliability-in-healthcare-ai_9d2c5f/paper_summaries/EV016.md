# EV016: Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI): Concepts, taxonomies, opportunities and challenges toward responsible AI

Year: 2019

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.inffus.2019.12.012

## Core Claim

This paper provides a comprehensive review of Explainable AI concepts, develops a taxonomy of XAI approaches, and identifies opportunities and challenges for responsible AI development.

## Method Or Focus

- Systematic literature review
- Taxonomy development
- Conceptual analysis
- Comparative analysis of XAI approaches

## Limitations To Track

- Lack of standardized evaluation metrics for XAI
- Scalability issues in explaining complex models
- Trade-offs between model performance and explainability

## Possible Gap

There is a need for standardized evaluation frameworks and benchmarks for XAI methods across different domains and model types.
