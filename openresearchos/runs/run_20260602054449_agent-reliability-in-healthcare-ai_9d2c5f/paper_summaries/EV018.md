# EV018: Review of deep learning: concepts, CNN architectures, challenges, applications, future directions

Year: 2021

URL: https://doi.org/10.1186/s40537-021-00444-8

## Core Claim

This paper provides a comprehensive review of deep learning using a holistic approach to offer a more complete understanding of DL, covering concepts, architectures, challenges, applications, and future directions.

## Method Or Focus

- Deep learning (DL)
- Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
- CNN architectures (AlexNet, HR.Net)
- Computational tools (FPGA, GPU, CPU)

## Limitations To Track

- Previous reviews only tackled one aspect of DL, leading to an overall lack of knowledge
- Existing research gaps and challenges in the field

## Possible Gap

The paper reveals that previous DL reviews were too narrow, focusing on single aspects rather than providing a holistic view of the field, which limits comprehensive understanding.
