# EV017: PRISMA 2020 explanation and elaboration: updated guidance and exemplars for reporting systematic reviews

Year: 2021

URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmj.n160

## Core Claim

The PRISMA 2020 statement provides updated guidance and exemplars for reporting systematic reviews, including a checklist of 27 items with explanations and bullet points for detailed recommendations to improve transparency, completeness, and accuracy of reporting.

## Method Or Focus

- Explanation and elaboration of reporting guidelines
- Bullet points for detailed reporting recommendations
- Examples from published reviews

## Limitations To Track

- No evaluation of PRISMA 2020 effectiveness in improving reporting quality
- No discussion of implementation challenges

## Possible Gap

The paper doesn't address how to measure the impact of PRISMA 2020 on actual reporting practices or identify barriers to implementation of the updated guidelines.
