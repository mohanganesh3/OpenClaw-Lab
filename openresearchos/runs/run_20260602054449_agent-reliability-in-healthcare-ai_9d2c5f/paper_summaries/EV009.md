# EV009: AgentClinic: a multimodal benchmark for tool-using clinical AI agents

Year: unknown

URL: https://agentclinic.github.io/

## Core Claim

AgentClinic is introduced as a multimodal agent benchmark for evaluating LLMs in simulated clinical environments that includes patient interactions, multimodal data collection under incomplete information, and tool usage across nine medical specialties and seven languages.

## Method Or Focus

- Multimodal agent benchmark design
- Simulated clinical environments
- Patient interaction modeling
- Multimodal data collection under incomplete information
- Tool usage evaluation

## Limitations To Track

- Simulated environments may not fully capture real clinical complexity
- Incomplete information scenarios may not represent all clinical challenges
- The benchmark may not cover all medical specialties or languages

## Possible Gap

There is a lack of comprehensive evaluation frameworks for LLMs in clinical settings that require tool usage, patient interaction, and handling incomplete information across multiple medical specialties and languages.
