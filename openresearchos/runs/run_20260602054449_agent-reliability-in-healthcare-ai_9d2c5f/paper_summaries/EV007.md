## Core Claim

This repository provides a curated, comprehensive list of research papers, projects, and resources on the application of Agentic AI and AI agents in healthcare.

## Method Or Focus

- Curation and organization of existing literature
- Systematic categorization of resources by application domain
- Compilation of a comprehensive bibliography and resource list

## Limitations To Track

- The repository itself is a list, not a primary research contribution, and thus lacks novel experimental validation.
- The quality and completeness of the curated list depend on the maintainers' ongoing efforts and community contributions.

## Possible Gap

The repository highlights a gap in consolidated, agent-based solutions for healthcare administration and patient dialogue, suggesting a need for more integrated and deployable agent systems in these specific areas.
