# EV020: Opinion Paper: “So what if ChatGPT wrote it?” Multidisciplinary perspectives on opportunities, challenges and implications of generative conversational AI for research, practice and policy

Year: 2023

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijinfomgt.2023.102642

## Core Claim

This paper provides multidisciplinary perspectives on ChatGPT and generative conversational AI, highlighting both opportunities and challenges across various fields, while identifying areas for further research.

## Method Or Focus

- Literature review
- Expert opinion synthesis
- Multi-disciplinary analysis

## Limitations To Track

- Disruptions to existing practices
- Threats to privacy and security
- Consequences of biases, misuse, and misinformation

## Possible Gap

The paper reveals a gap in understanding the optimal combinations of human and generative AI for various tasks and identifying ways to assess the accuracy of text produced by generative AI.
