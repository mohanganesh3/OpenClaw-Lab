# EV019: Digital transformation: A multidisciplinary reflection and research agenda

Year: 2019

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbusres.2019.09.022

## Core Claim

The paper identifies three stages of digital transformation (digitization, digitalization, and digital transformation), growth strategies for digital firms, required assets and capabilities, organizational structures, and consequences for performance metrics, while providing a research agenda for future studies.

## Method Or Focus

- Literature review
- Identification and delineation of concepts
- Development of research agenda

## Limitations To Track

- Need for a research agenda indicates many open questions
- Limited understanding of how organizational structures affect transformation
- Unclear how performance metrics should be adapted

## Possible Gap

The paper reveals a gap in understanding how specific organizational structures and adapted performance metrics contribute to successful digital transformation.
