## Core Claim

This paper introduces a novel framework leveraging Large Reasoning MultiModals (LRMs) with an attention-guided ensemble architecture and context engineering to solve text extraction in turbulence-blurred images.

## Limitations

- Traditional OCR and standard VLMs suffer from very low accuracy (6-15%) and are fundamentally limited by pattern-matching for severely distorted images.
