## Core Claim

The study uses machine learning models (WATCH and TRIGGER) trained on Fermi-LAT light curves with 42 variability features from 365-day trailing windows to predict gamma-ray blazar flares within 90 days (WATCH) and 45 days (TRIGGER).

## Limitations

- No reliable pre-onset TRIGGER alert is obtained
- TRIGGER AP = 0.123 is relatively low
