# EV005: AIBuildAI-2: Scalable Agentic AI Research and Engineering

Year: 2026

URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27873

## Core Claim

AIBuildAI-2 presents a framework for AI systems that can autonomously build and improve other AI systems through multi-agent research and engineering loops with traceability requirements.

## Method Or Focus

- Multi-agent systems for AI research and engineering
- Autonomous improvement loops for AI systems
- Traceability mechanisms for AI system evolution
- Scalable engineering approaches for AI development

## Limitations To Track

- Scalability challenges in complex multi-agent systems
- Traceability maintenance in autonomous improvement loops
- Potential for error propagation in self-improving systems

## Possible Gap

The paper focuses on the framework for AI systems that build and improve other AI systems but may not address how to ensure the quality, safety, and ethical alignment of these self-improving systems.
