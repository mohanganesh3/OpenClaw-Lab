## Core Claim

This paper addresses the problem of long-tailed distributions in graph-level classification, where standard GNNs are biased towards head classes and fail to generalize on tail classes.

## Limitations

- Existing methods assume a balanced class distribution, which is unrealistic for real-world data.
- Predictions from existing algorithms are often not trustworthy.
