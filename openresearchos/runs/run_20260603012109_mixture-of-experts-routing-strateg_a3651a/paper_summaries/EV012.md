## Core Claim

A welding simulation for seam tracking based on a purpose-built vision sensor is designed to improve the welding efficiency of robotic Gas Metal Arc Welding (GMAW).

## Limitations

- The system's performance is sensitive to groove styles, which may limit its applicability to unseen or highly complex weld geometries.
