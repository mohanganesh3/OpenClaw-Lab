## Abstract

With the rapid development of drone technology and artificial intelligence algorithms, innovatively applying them to maritime inspection and river water quality management is an important direction to improve water information management. With its unique advantages, drones are able to conduct full-coverage inspections of river water and detect abnormalities and potential dangers in a timely and sensitive manner. Combining drone technology with artificial intelligence algorithms can replace the traditional manual inspection mode and realize the real-time and efficient processing of drone feedback images and dam monitoring images. Through the intelligent recognition system, the behavior of people in the reservoir area and dam hazards can be automatically and accurately identified. Once an abnormality is detected, the system will immediately activate the tracking mechanism to dissuade relevant personnel, quickly report hazards, issue timely warnings, and provide solid early support for subsequent experts to carry out emergency response work. During the inspection process, the system also has the ability to dynamically plan routes and reach areas that are difficult to reach by manual inspection. At the same time, interference detection and airspace monitoring further enhance the ability to safeguard the safe operation of river water bodies in all aspects, laying a solid foundation for the stable management of water bodies.
