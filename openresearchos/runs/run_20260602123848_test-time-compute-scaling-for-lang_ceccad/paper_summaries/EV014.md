## Core Claim

This paper introduces a novel framework using Large Reasoning MultiModals and an attention-guided ensemble architecture to significantly improve text extraction from images degraded by atmospheric turbulence.

## Limitations

- Standard vision-language models (VLMs) are fundamentally limited by their pattern-matching approach, leading to poor accuracy (9-15%) on severely distorted text.
