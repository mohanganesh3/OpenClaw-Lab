## Abstract

Blockchain was thought of as a revolutionary platform with implementation potential in diverse industries. Thus, different blockchain networks have been implemented to analyze their utility in multiple scenarios. In this article, a comprehensive assessment of blockchain efficiency by classifying all the solutions into two broad categories; an empirical study and computational modeling is conducted. The latest methodological blockchain approach includes benchmarking, testing, experimental research and then modeling. It is comparatively tested for stochastic models of computational modeling which is used to test the efficiency of conventional blockchain consensus algorithms. By contrasting, comparing, and grouping the various approaches together, significant parameters can be used to choose the most effective measurement technology to maximize blockchain device performance based on the detection of the bottlenecks. Finally, the proposed approach lists suggested recommendations for possible inquiries.
