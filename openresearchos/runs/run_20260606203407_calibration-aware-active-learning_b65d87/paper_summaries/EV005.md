## Problem
Developing AI models remains a heavily manual process that is challenging for non-AI experts, and existing agents are limited by static, outdated, and sparse parametric knowledge in their underlying LLMs.

## Core Claim
AIBuildAI-2, a knowledge-enhanced agent with an evolving hierarchical knowledge system, achieves expert-level performance in autonomous AI model development by dynamically loading relevant context and continuously improving from experience.

## Key Contributions
- Hierarchical knowledge system organizing curated AI development documents into high-level instructions and low-level documents
- Dynamic context loading mechanism that grounds decisions in verifiable expertise
- Continual evolution of knowledge system through distillation of completed runs into structured takeaways
- Superior performance on MLE-Bench (70.7% medal rate) and top-tier human expert performance in Kaggle competitions

## Method
AIBuildAI-2 initializes by collecting and organizing AI development documents into a hierarchical knowledge system, then dynamically loads relevant context for each AI task, grounds decisions in verifiable expertise, and continually evolves by distilling completed runs into structured knowledge updates.
- Components: Hierarchical knowledge organization with high-level instructions and low-level documents, Dynamic context loading based on current state and AI task, Knowledge distillation from completed runs into structured takeaways, External verification of expertise through curated documents
- Key innovation: The evolving hierarchical knowledge system that continuously improves from agent experience by distilling completed runs into structured takeaways, addressing the static knowledge limitation of prior agents.
- Assumptions: Curated web documents provide sufficient initial knowledge for AI development; Distilling completed runs yields valuable structured knowledge updates; Dynamic context loading can effectively identify relevant expertise for each task
