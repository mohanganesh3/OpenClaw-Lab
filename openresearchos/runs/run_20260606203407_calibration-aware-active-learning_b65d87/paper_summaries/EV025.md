## Critical Reading
- Stated limitations: The abstract doesn't explicitly state limitations; The approach is specifically designed for phase fraction prediction in RMPEAs, which may limit generalizability to other tasks
- Unstated weaknesses: The computational complexity of MDNs might be high; The abstract doesn't provide details on the active learning strategy; The paper doesn't address how the model handles extreme or rare compositions
- Threats to validity: The model might overfit to the specific RMPEA compositions in the training set; The uncertainty quantification might not be well-calibrated across the entire temperature range; The active learning approach might introduce bias in the selection of out-of-domain samples
- Open questions: How well does the uncertainty quantification perform across different alloy systems?; Can the active learning approach be adapted to other domains beyond materials science?; How does the computational cost scale with the complexity of the alloy systems?
