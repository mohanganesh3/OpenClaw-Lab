## Critical Reading
- Stated limitations: The paper addresses limitations of existing machine learning approaches that rely on deterministic mappings and neglect uncertainty in predictions
- Unstated weaknesses: The paper doesn't explicitly address noisy labels which is a key concern in the project; Domain specificity to materials science may limit direct applicability to medical data; Unclear performance with very small datasets
- Threats to validity: Domain specificity may limit generalization to medical tabular data; Uncertainty quantification focuses on aleatoric uncertainty but may not fully address epistemic uncertainty; Lack of explicit handling of noisy labels
- Open questions: How does the MDN approach perform with very small datasets?; Can the uncertainty quantification methods be effectively used for calibration?; How does the approach handle noisy labels?
