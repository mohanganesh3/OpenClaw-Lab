## Problem
Scientific discovery is lengthy and costly, limiting researchers from exploring many high-quality ideas due to resource and time constraints.

## Core Claim
Agent Laboratory can accelerate scientific discovery by reducing research costs and improving quality through an autonomous LLM-based framework that completes the entire research process.

## Key Contributions
- Introduction of Agent Laboratory framework capable of completing entire research process from idea to publication
- Demonstration that o1-preview generates best research outcomes, with human involvement significantly improving quality and 84% cost reduction

## Method
Agent Laboratory accepts a human research idea and progresses through three stages—literature review, experimentation, and report writing—using specialized LLM agents, while enabling users to provide feedback and guidance at each stage to produce comprehensive research outputs.
- Components: Literature review agents, Experimentation agents, Report writing agents, Human feedback integration mechanism
- Key innovation: The specialized agent pipeline that can autonomously execute the entire research process from idea to publication-ready outputs, with the ability to incorporate human guidance at each stage to improve quality.
- Assumptions: State-of-the-art LLMs can effectively perform research tasks; Human feedback can meaningfully improve research outputs; Generated machine learning code can achieve competitive performance
