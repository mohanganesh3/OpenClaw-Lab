## Problem
Developing AI models remains a heavily manual process requiring specialized expertise, and existing AI development agents have limited performance due to static, outdated, and sparse knowledge in their underlying large language models.

## Core Claim
AIBuildAI-2, a knowledge-enhanced agent with an external, evolving knowledge system, significantly improves AI model development capabilities to match expert human practitioners.

## Key Contributions
- AIBuildAI-2 introduces a hierarchical knowledge system organizing curated AI development knowledge into high-level instructions and low-level documents
- The system evolves from the agent's own experience by distilling completed runs into structured takeaways written back into the knowledge system
- Achieves state-of-the-art performance on MLE-Bench (70.7% medal rate) and competes at top-tier human levels in real-world Kaggle competitions

## Method
AIBuildAI-2 uses a hierarchical knowledge system that dynamically loads relevant context based on task state and current progress, with knowledge continuously evolving from completed runs through structured distillation.
- Components: Hierarchical knowledge system with topical categories and curated documents, Dynamic context loading mechanism based on task state and requirements, Experience distillation process converting completed runs into structured knowledge takeaways
- Key innovation: The continuously evolving external knowledge system that addresses the static knowledge limitation of prior agents by learning from its own experience and maintaining up-to-date expertise.
- Assumptions: AI development knowledge can be effectively organized into hierarchical categories; Experience distillation yields structured, reusable knowledge components; Relevant knowledge can be dynamically identified and loaded based on task context
