## Core Claim

Agent Laboratory is a framework that uses LLM agents to automate the research process in three stages: literature review, experimentation, and report writing, based on a human-provided research idea.

## Limitations

- Human involvement, providing feedback at each stage, significantly improves the overall quality of research.
