## Core Claim

The paper presents a socio-technical analysis of agentic AI using the MAD-BAD-SAD construct as an analytical lens, examining how architectural choices in perception, cognition, planning, execution, and memory introduce dependencies related to data governance, accountability, transparency, safety, and sustainability.

## Limitations

- Societal, ethical, economic, environmental, and governance implications of agentic AI remain weakly integrated into technical treatments
