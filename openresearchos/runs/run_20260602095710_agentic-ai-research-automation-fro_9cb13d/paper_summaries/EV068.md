## Core Claim

AgentBench is a multi-dimensional benchmark designed to evaluate LLM-as-Agent across a spectrum of different environments, encompassing eight distinct environments categorized into three types of groundings: Code, Game, and Web.

## Limitations

- Lack of a systematic and standard benchmark to evaluate LLM-as-Agent
- Previous text-based game environments suffer from closed, discrete action spaces
- Previous embodied agent simulators don't accurately reflect practical use cases of LLMs
- Most benchmarks focus on single environments and fail to provide comprehensive overview
