## Core Claim

Introduces Moment-Video, a benchmark with 1,000 human-verified video-QA pairs across 7 domains and 25 subcategories, covering four task types: Temporal Occurrence, Temporal Counting, Action Description, and Temporal Reasoning.

## Limitations

- Denser frame sampling improves some models but does not eliminate the bottleneck
- Longer videos introduce stronger temporal-localization challenges
- Current video MLLMs lack temporally faithful representations for capturing, preserving, and using brief but decisive visual evidence
