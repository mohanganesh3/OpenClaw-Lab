## Core Claim

ClinEnv is an interactive benchmark that evaluates LLMs as attending physicians through Longitudinal Inpatient Simulation, where models must query four specialized agents at each decision stage before committing to medications, procedures, and diagnoses.

## Limitations

- Models struggle with management decisions
- Models continue to issue redundant queries as cases progress
- Information-acquisition gap is invisible to outcome-only evaluation
