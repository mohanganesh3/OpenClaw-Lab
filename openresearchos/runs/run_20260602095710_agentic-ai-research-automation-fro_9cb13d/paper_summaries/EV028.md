## Core Claim

This paper presents a survey and framework for AutoResearch, analyzing the spectrum of AI-powered scientific workflow automation from human-steered 'Vibe Research' to fully autonomous AI-led systems.

## Limitations

- AutoResearch autonomy is domain-conditioned, being more credible in structured, executable, and rapidly verifiable settings but limited in embodied, delayed, heterogeneous, ethical, or institutionally accountable contexts.
