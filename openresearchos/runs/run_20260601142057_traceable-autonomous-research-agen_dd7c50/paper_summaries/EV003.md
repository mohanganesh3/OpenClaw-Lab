# EV003: Agent Laboratory: Using LLM Agents as Research Assistants

Year: 2025

URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2501.04227

## Core Claim

The paper describes using LLM agents as research assistants across literature review, experimentation, and report writing, highlighting the need for human oversight and reproducibility controls.

## Method Or Focus

- Agent-based research assistance
- LLM-powered literature review
- Automated experimentation
- Report generation
- Human oversight mechanisms
- Reproducibility controls

## Limitations To Track

- Need for human oversight (implying current systems may lack sufficient autonomy or reliability)
- Reproducibility challenges
- Potential for errors or hallucinations in LLM outputs

## Possible Gap

The paper reveals the gap between current LLM capabilities and fully autonomous research assistance, particularly in ensuring reproducibility and maintaining scientific rigor without human intervention.
