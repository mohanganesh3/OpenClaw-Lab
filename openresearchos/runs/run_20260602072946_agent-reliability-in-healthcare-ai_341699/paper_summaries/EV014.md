# EV014: Impacts of epidemic outbreaks on supply chains: mapping a research agenda amid the COVID-19 pandemic through a structured literature review

Year: 2020

URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/s10479-020-03685-7

## Core Claim

The study proposes a framework for operations and supply chain management during the COVID-19 pandemic, identifying six key perspectives (adaptation, digitalization, preparedness, recovery, ripple effect, sustainability) and highlighting research gaps in commercial supply chains.

## Method Or Focus

- structured literature review
- systematic analysis
- content gap analysis

## Limitations To Track

- focus on commercial supply chains may overlook humanitarian logistics insights
- potential bias in literature selection
- limited empirical data from early pandemic phases

## Possible Gap

The paper identifies a lack of research on long-term sustainability and systemic ripple effects in global supply chains during prolonged epidemics.
