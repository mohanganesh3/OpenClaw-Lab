# EV002: PRISMA 2020 explanation and elaboration: updated guidance and exemplars for reporting systematic reviews

Year: 2021

URL: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmj.n160

## Core Claim

The PRISMA 2020 statement provides updated guidance with a 27-item checklist to improve transparency, completeness, and accuracy in reporting systematic reviews through explanations, bullet points, and exemplars.

## Method Or Focus

- Checklist development
- Explanatory recommendations
- Published review exemplars
- Reporting standardization

## Limitations To Track

- No empirical validation of checklist effectiveness
- Dependence on voluntary adoption
- No mention of automated implementation tools

## Possible Gap

The paper does not address challenges in implementing the PRISMA 2020 checklist in non-English or low-resource research contexts.
