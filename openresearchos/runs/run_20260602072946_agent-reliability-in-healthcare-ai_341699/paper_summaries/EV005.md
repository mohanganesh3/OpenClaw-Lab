# EV005: Opinion Paper: “So what if ChatGPT wrote it?” Multidisciplinary perspectives on opportunities, challenges and implications of generative conversational AI for research, practice and policy

Year: 2023

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijinfomgt.2023.102642

## Core Claim

The paper synthesizes multidisciplinary perspectives on ChatGPT's opportunities (e.g., productivity gains in banking, hospitality, IT) and challenges (e.g., ethics, security, bias) across research, practice, and policy, identifying gaps in knowledge, transparency, and digital transformation.

## Method Or Focus

- expert contributions
- case study analysis
- thematic analysis
- literature review

## Limitations To Track

- Lack of consensus on regulation
- Potential biases in AI outputs
- Privacy and security risks

## Possible Gap

The paper highlights unresolved questions about balancing innovation with ethical/legal safeguards, particularly in cross-sectoral applications and regulatory frameworks.
