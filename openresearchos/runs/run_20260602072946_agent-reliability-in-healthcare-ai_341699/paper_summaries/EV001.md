# EV001: Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI): Concepts, taxonomies, opportunities and challenges toward responsible AI

Year: 2019

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.inffus.2019.12.012

## Core Claim

The paper provides a comprehensive overview of Explainable AI (XAI), including concepts, taxonomies, opportunities, and challenges, to advance responsible AI development.

## Method Or Focus

- Literature review
- Taxonomy development
- Case studies analysis
- Challenges identification
- Future directions exploration

## Limitations To Track

- Lack of standardized evaluation frameworks for XAI methods
- Trade-offs between model complexity and interpretability
- Limited real-world deployment case studies

## Possible Gap

The paper highlights the need for standardized evaluation frameworks to systematically compare XAI techniques across different AI applications and stakeholders.
