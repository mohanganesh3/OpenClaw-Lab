# EV013: The Role of ChatGPT, Generative Language Models, and Artificial Intelligence in Medical Education: A Conversation With ChatGPT and a Call for Papers

Year: 2023

URL: https://doi.org/10.2196/46885

## Core Claim

The paper explores ChatGPT's capabilities and potential in medical education through an interview, highlighting its applications in generating educational content and tools while addressing limitations like hallucinations and the need for proper prompting.

## Method Or Focus

- virtual patient simulation generation
- quiz generation
- simulated doctor-patient communication critique
- research article summarization
- academic integrity detection methods discussion
- AI curriculum development
- call for papers drafting

## Limitations To Track

- hallucinations and occasional factual inaccuracies
- dependence on proper prompting for accurate output
- lack of robust detection methods for machine-generated text

## Possible Gap

The paper implies a need for improved detection methods to identify machine-generated text and ensure academic integrity in medical education contexts.
