# EV007: AI4People—An Ethical Framework for a Good AI Society: Opportunities, Risks, Principles, and Recommendations

Year: 2018

URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/s11023-018-9482-5

## Core Claim

The paper introduces the AI4People initiative, outlining opportunities, risks, ethical principles, and 20 recommendations for developing a 'Good AI Society' through multidisciplinary collaboration and stakeholder engagement.

## Method Or Focus

- Literature review
- Ethical analysis
- Stakeholder consultations
- Policy framework development
- Principles synthesis

## Limitations To Track

- Lack of implementation case studies
- Dependence on stakeholder compliance
- No quantitative validation of proposed principles

## Possible Gap

The paper does not address potential conflicts between ethical principles or provide mechanisms for resolving disputes in AI governance.
