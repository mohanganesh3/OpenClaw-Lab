# EV010: Guidelines for the Prevention of Intravascular Catheter-related Infections

Year: 2011

URL: https://doi.org/10.1093/cid/cir257

## Core Claim

These guidelines provide evidence-based recommendations for preventing intravascular catheter-related infections across healthcare settings, developed through multidisciplinary expert collaboration.

## Method Or Focus

- Multidisciplinary working group formation
- Evidence synthesis from existing literature
- Development of consensus recommendations

## Limitations To Track

- No explicit mention of implementation challenges in home healthcare settings
- Lack of specific data on cost-effectiveness of recommendations

## Possible Gap

The guidelines do not address emerging technologies like antimicrobial-coated catheters or AI-based infection surveillance systems, leaving a gap in modern prevention strategies.
