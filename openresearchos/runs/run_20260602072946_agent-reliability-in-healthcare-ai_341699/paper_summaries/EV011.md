# EV011: ChatGPT Utility in Healthcare Education, Research, and Practice: Systematic Review on the Promising Perspectives and Valid Concerns

Year: 2023

URL: https://doi.org/10.3390/healthcare11060887

## Core Claim

The systematic review evaluates ChatGPT's benefits (85% of studies) in healthcare education, research, and practice, while highlighting concerns (96.7% of studies) like ethical, copyright, and transparency issues.

## Method Or Focus

- Systematic review using PRISMA guidelines
- Search of PubMed/MEDLINE and Google Scholar
- Inclusion of 60 eligible English records

## Limitations To Track

- Focus on English-language studies only
- No direct assessment of ChatGPT performance
- Potential bias from preprint inclusion

## Possible Gap

The review does not deeply explore solutions to mitigate identified concerns or compare ChatGPT with other AI models in healthcare contexts.
