# EV020: A Survey on Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI): Toward Medical XAI

Year: 2020

URL: https://doi.org/10.1109/tnnls.2020.3027314

## Core Claim

The paper provides a survey and categorization of explainable AI (XAI) methods, focusing on their application to medical contexts to enhance transparency and accountability in clinical decision-making.

## Method Or Focus

- Rule-based explanations
- Feature importance analysis
- Attention mechanisms
- Visualization techniques
- Model-agnostic interpretability methods

## Limitations To Track

- Blackbox nature of deep learning remains unresolved
- Lack of standardized evaluation frameworks for medical XAI
- Limited integration of domain-specific medical knowledge into XAI frameworks

## Possible Gap

The paper highlights the need for standardized evaluation metrics and domain-specific adaptations of XAI methods in medical applications, which remain underexplored.
