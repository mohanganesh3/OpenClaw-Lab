# EV006: Artificial Intelligence (AI): Multidisciplinary perspectives on emerging challenges, opportunities, and agenda for research, practice and policy

Year: 2019

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijinfomgt.2019.08.002

## Core Claim

The paper synthesizes multidisciplinary perspectives on AI's transformative potential across industries and society, emphasizing opportunities, challenges, and research agendas while highlighting the interplay between technological development and societal influence.

## Method Or Focus

- Expert contributions
- Literature review
- Cross-domain analysis

## Limitations To Track

- Lack of empirical data
- No specific case studies
- No quantitative evaluation metrics

## Possible Gap

The paper identifies a need for deeper empirical research on AI's societal and industrial impacts, particularly in standardizing evaluation metrics and addressing ethical implications across domains.
