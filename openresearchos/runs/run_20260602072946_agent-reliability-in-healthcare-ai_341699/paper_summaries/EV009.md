# EV009: Interpreting Black-Box Models: A Review on Explainable Artificial Intelligence

Year: 2023

URL: https://doi.org/10.1007/s12559-023-10179-8

## Core Claim

This study provides a comprehensive review of explainable AI (XAI) models, analyzing their development, evaluating frameworks, and highlighting emerging challenges to guide applied and theoretical research.

## Method Or Focus

- Literature review
- Comparative analysis of XAI frameworks
- Identification of open challenges

## Limitations To Track

- Difficulty in efficiently identifying flaws in black-box models
- Lack of standardized evaluation metrics for XAI methods
- Unresolved challenges in balancing model complexity and interpretability

## Possible Gap

The paper highlights unresolved challenges in developing efficient methods to detect flaws in black-box models and standardize XAI framework evaluation, but does not propose concrete solutions.
