# EV016: Artificial intelligence in supply chain management: A systematic literature review

Year: 2020

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbusres.2020.09.009

## Core Claim

This paper identifies current and potential AI techniques in supply chain management through a systematic literature review, analyzing prevalent AI applications, underutilized techniques, and high-potential subfields while identifying research gaps.

## Method Or Focus

- Systematic literature review
- Inclusion/exclusion criteria
- Analysis of four SCM subfields (logistics, marketing, supply chain, production)

## Limitations To Track

- Focus on four specific SCM subfields may limit comprehensiveness
- No explicit mention of time frame for literature inclusion
- Potential bias from exclusion criteria

## Possible Gap

The paper highlights gaps in under-researched AI techniques and subfields with high potential for AI enhancement but does not prioritize or propose frameworks for addressing these gaps.
