# EV018: ChatGPT and Open-AI Models: A Preliminary Review

Year: 2023

URL: https://doi.org/10.3390/fi15060192

## Core Claim

This paper provides the first comprehensive literature review of ChatGPT at the time of publication, summarizing its training process, functionality, and potential implications while identifying challenges.

## Method Or Focus

- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Supervised Learning
- Reinforcement Learning

## Limitations To Track

- Limited empirical evaluation of long-term implications
- Dependence on proprietary training data
- Preliminary nature of the review

## Possible Gap

The paper highlights the need for further research on addressing challenges like ethical implications and long-term societal impacts of ChatGPT, which remain underexplored.
