# EV004: Digital transformation: A multidisciplinary reflection and research agenda

Year: 2019

URL: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbusres.2019.09.022

## Core Claim

The paper identifies three stages of digital transformation (digitization, digitalization, digital transformation), outlines growth strategies and required assets/capabilities for digital firms, and proposes organizational structures and performance metrics for successful transformation, while providing a research agenda.

## Method Or Focus

- Literature review
- Conceptual analysis
- Theoretical framework development

## Limitations To Track

- Lack of empirical validation
- Focus on theoretical aspects without case studies
- Need for interdisciplinary empirical research

## Possible Gap

The paper calls for future research to explore the interplay between digital transformation stages and organizational structures across industries, particularly in understudied sectors.
