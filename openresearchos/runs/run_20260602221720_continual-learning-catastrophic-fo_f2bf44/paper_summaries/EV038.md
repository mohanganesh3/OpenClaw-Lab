## Core Claim

Unsupervised continual learning (UCL) aims to develop learning systems that can acquire knowledge from a sequence of unlabeled and potentially non-stationary data while retaining previously learned information, without relying on task labels or explicit supervision.

## Limitations

- Difficulty in retaining previously learned information on non-stationary, unlabeled data.
