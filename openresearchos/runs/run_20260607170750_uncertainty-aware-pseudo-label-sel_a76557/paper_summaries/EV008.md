## Experimental Setup
- Datasets: ACDC (200 cardiac MRI images from 100 patients), ISIC (1838 training images, 756 validation images), PROMISE12 (50 patients, converted to 2D slices)
- Baselines: MT, UA-MT, EM, DCT, CCT, CPS, ICT, DAN, URPC, SSNet, ICT-Med, FixMatch, SCPNet, BCP-Net, CTCT, S4CVnet, MedFCT, CnT-B, DiffRect, CPC-SAM
- Metrics: Dice Coefficient (DSC), 95% Hausdorff Distance (95HD), Average Symmetric Surface Distance (ASSD)
- Compute: single GPU

## Results
Headline: SemSim achieved 0.896 DSC and 2.3mm 95HD on ACDC with 10% labeled data

| Method | Dataset | Metric | Value |

| SemSim | ACDC | DSC | 0.896 |

- Ablations: Table IV: All three consistency constraints (intra, cross, unsupervised) needed for optimal performance (0.872 DSC vs 0.844 without cross-image); Table V: Optimal sub-regions N=4 (0.896 DSC) vs N=1 (0.888 DSC) on ACDC; Spatial-aware fusion module (SFM) contributes 0.5% DSC improvement (0.861 vs 0.872)
- Improvement over baseline: SemSim outperforms CPC-SAM by +0.6% DSC and -0.2mm 95HD on ACDC (10% labeled), +0.8% DSC and -5.6mm 95HD on ISIC (10% labeled), and +5.8% DSC on PROMISE12 (7% labeled)
