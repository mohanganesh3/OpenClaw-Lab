## Core Claim

The paper presents a multi-tier labeling cascade that combines three weak supervision sources (physics rule set, IMM-UKF bank, and supplemental-element calibration) to produce labels at scale, followed by training a 6.5M-parameter Transformer in two stages for orbital anomaly detection.

## Limitations

- The model is framed as a high-recall triage classifier for surfacing candidate events, not for issuing final attributions
