## Core Claim

An encoder-decoder network (MSGCNet) is proposed to address the challenges of scale and appearance variance in semantic segmentation of high-resolution remote sensing images.

## Limitations

- Deep convolutional neural networks (DCNNs) have inherent limitations in capturing global context.
- Attention mechanisms and transformers result in high computational costs for high-resolution images.
