## Core Claim

This paper develops a methodology to directly elicit the AV behavior pedestrians find suitable by collecting quantitative data to measure and improve algorithm performance.

## Limitations

- The methodology is limited by its use of a simple, simulated Pygame environment and feedback collected in a controlled setting, which may not reflect real-world complexity.
