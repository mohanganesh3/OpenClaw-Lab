## Core Claim

Neural representations in the brain dynamically evolve during learning, transitioning from abstract, task-structure-focused codes to more efficient, action-oriented codes.

## Limitations

- Findings are based on non-human primate models, limiting direct generalization to humans.
- The study is correlational; it does not establish a causal mechanism for the observed representational shifts.
