## Core Claim

Intrinsic Human-In-The-Loop Reinforcement Learning (HITL-RL) using EEG headsets can accelerate training convergence of RL algorithms while reducing the human burden.

## Limitations

- The system's effectiveness is dependent on the human user's ability to consistently detect and generate error-related potentials (ErrP).
