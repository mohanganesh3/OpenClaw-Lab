## Abstract


 This paper presents a novel Agentic AI-powered enterprise solution that systematizes the identification, evaluation, prioritization, and orchestration of emerging technologies against concrete business challenges in the energy sector. Leveraging a multi-agent GenAI architecture comprising, Technology Detector, Business Expert, Tech Orchestrator, and Tech Producer Agents, the solution continuously harvests global technology intelligence from research outputs, vendor ecosystems, standards developments, and digital discourse. Domain-aligned reasoning, hierarchical decision frameworks, reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), and an enterprise impact assessment matrix converge to generate actionable, context-rich recommendations. Pilot deployments across upstream, downstream and capital projects environments demonstrated substantial reductions in discovery life cycle, improved cross-functional convergence, and more evidence-based technology investment decisions. Furthermore, enhancing competitiveness, operational resilience, and sustainability trajectories. The architecture dissolves traditional silos, enabling adaptive, strategic technology adoption in complex operational environments.
